Here are some different ways you can help to support us in addition, of course, to seeing a play!

Become a member

Support us by getting involved directly. See the join us page.

Join mailing list

Enter your email address in the See a play box. You will then receive details of upcoming productions and any other news.

Location Hunt

We are currently without a permanent base and are actively looking at possible locations. If you are aware of any locations both for rehearsals and performance space, please get in contact at {{ site.social_links.email }}.

Easy Fundraising

When shopping online have a look if you can purchase via Easy Fundraising. This service allows us to receive donations from online retailers at no extra cost to you.

Donate

Donations are always welcome. We would also appreciate items which we can use in our performances such as costumes and vintage furniture.
