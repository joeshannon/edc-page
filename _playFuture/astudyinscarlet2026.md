### By {{ page.author }}
### Directed by {{ page.director }}

The game is afoot in this adaptation of Arthur Conan Doyle’s novel which
introduced the world to Sherlock Holmes and Dr Watson.

In 1881, Dr John H Watson, newly returned from war, is introduced to the
eccentric Sherlock Holmes and drawn into a mystery where a dead body is found
with no apparent injury and the only clue to the murder being the word ‘RACHE’
scrawled above it, leaving the police without a clue.

Our intrepid duo ends up unfurling a tale spanning two continents and numerous
twists, in this debut tale of one of literature’s best known detectives and his
companion, adapted with a comedic touch for the stage by Bart Lovins.
